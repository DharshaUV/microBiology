Short-term & Long-term internships

Online internship
  • Topics will be posted in the website time to time covering all research perspectives.
  • Students acquire virtual knowledge which compensates the hands-on experimentation.
  • Experiment basics will be covered initially following live practical classes
  • Assessment of understanding will be done through quick questions at the end of session and submission of short notes/MCQs after the session
  • The student will be provided an e-certificate after successful completion of classes and notes.
Hands-on internship
  • The students can fix their slots based on their convenient timings.
  • The experiments will be performed in-person/groups following lab safety guidelines.
  • Attention to each individual is our primary concern during experimentation.
  • Separate theory class with video demonstration (if required) followed by live practical class.
  • Assessment of understanding will be done through submission of brief notes after the session.
  • The student will be provided a certificate after successful completion of classes and notes.

Final year Projects & Mini projects

Heading
  • The students can frame their own projects based on the research areas under study
  • Assistance & guidance for execution will be provided throughout the project duration by trained experts.
  • Thesis and project documentation will also be tracked during the time course of the project
  • If needed and interested, the articles will be published in renowned journals.
For research scholars
Ph.D project
  • Lab facilities, project guidance and support will be provided until the successful completion of the work.
  • Dissertation works like thesis, synopsis, research and review article guidance and documentation will be facilitated.
  • Assistance and documentation of the research article publication in UGC-listed journals with good impact factor.
